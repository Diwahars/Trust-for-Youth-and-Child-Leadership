ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITES OF MENTOR

Mentor must be willing to accept the commitment of time and energy to provide support for a new mentee. A clear understanding of the mentoring process will go a long way towards assuring success. You will need to maintain support and open communication. You will also:

  • Intimate contact with the mentee
  • Meet mentee at least once in a month
  • Be a good listener
  • Meet at scheduled times
  • Maintain confidentiality
  • Give constructive feedback
  • Promote mentees skill and creativity
  • Share experience based cultural values
  • Help the mentee to move towards goal
  • Build a professional network for mentee
  • Maintain a positive attitude
  • Encourage independence
  • Create access to resources (Financial & Non - Financial)
  • Remember there is most often more than one way to reach a goal. Support the mentee to the best of your ability, but remember the project success or failure of the mentee is in the hands of the mentor.
